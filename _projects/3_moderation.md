This research project explores automated content moderation systems and techniques for detecting and mitigating misinformation across social media platforms. The work addresses one of the most pressing challenges in modern digital communication.

## Key Features

- **Automated Moderation**: Machine learning approaches to content classification
- **Misinformation Detection**: Techniques for identifying false or misleading information
- **Scalability**: Solutions designed for large-scale social media platforms
- **Ethical Considerations**: Balancing free speech with content safety

## Impact

As social media platforms continue to grow, the need for effective content moderation becomes increasingly critical. This research contributes to the development of automated systems that can help maintain platform safety while respecting user rights and platform scalability requirements.
