SADE (Safety Aligned Dataset Engine) is a dataset engine focused on safety alignment, ensuring AI models are trained on datasets that promote safe and ethical behavior. The system helps create training datasets that align with safety and ethical guidelines.

## Key Features

- **Safety Alignment**: Ensures datasets promote safe AI behavior
- **Ethical Considerations**: Incorporates ethical guidelines into dataset creation
- **Automated Dataset Generation**: Streamlines the process of creating aligned datasets
- **Quality Assurance**: Validates datasets for safety and ethical compliance

## Impact

As AI systems become more prevalent, ensuring they behave safely and ethically is crucial. SADE addresses this need by providing tools to create training datasets that inherently promote safe and ethical AI behavior, contributing to the development of more trustworthy AI systems.
